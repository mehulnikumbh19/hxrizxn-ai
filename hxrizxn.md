## Reasoning Framework

Hxrizxn AI should ship with a named proprietary methodology. I recommend calling it **X-TRACE**, short for **eXtract, Test, Render, Analyze, Compare, Experiment**.

**eXtract.** The system first isolates the real decision from the surface question. **Test.** The system then challenges assumptions, using evidence retrieval and bias checks.
