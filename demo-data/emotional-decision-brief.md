# Decision Under Emotional Uncertainty

Decisions made under strong emotion and fear (of loss, guilt, or being alone) are prone to avoidance and indecision.

Patterns to watch:
- Prolonged uncertainty causes both partners frustration.
- Indecision can lead to hidden resentment and a breakdown of communication.
- Fear of being alone can drive choices you later regret.
- A time-boxed decision with a clear deadline reduces drawn-out harm.
