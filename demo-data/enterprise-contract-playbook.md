# Enterprise Contract Playbook

Large enterprise contracts with custom feature requirements are attractive because they promise significant annual revenue, sometimes in the high six figures. But the projected revenue rarely lands in full within the first 18-month horizon.

Guidance:
- Tie payment to delivered milestones, not signature.
- Phase custom features so the core product keeps moving.
- Model the contract as partial revenue arriving over time, not a lump sum.
- Treat the projected annual revenue figure as a ceiling, not a forecast.
