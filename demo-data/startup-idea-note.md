# Rough Startup Idea Note

Build an AI workflow product for small compliance teams that turns policies, evidence requests, and audit tasks into a guided control-readiness cockpit.

Current evidence:
- Three informal conversations with former classmates in security and audit.
- One compliance manager said manual evidence chasing is painful.
- No paid pilot, letter of intent, or repeated weekly usage yet.

Fragile assumptions:
- Small teams have budget authority.
- The product can be narrow enough to ship in 30 days.
- Founder energy remains sustainable while working part-time.
